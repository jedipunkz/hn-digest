## Original Extract

Elia
Most AI gives you answers. Elia doesn't.
She asks questions — one at a time — to help you figure out what you actually think, want, or feel.
She's for the moments you're stuck in your head. A decision you can't make. A feeling you can't name. Something you keep circling.
She won't tell you what to do. She'll help you hear your own answer.
Not a therapist. If you're in real crisis, she'll point you to someone who can help.
