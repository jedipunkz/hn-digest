Context: Working as a senior software engineer in one of the big techs. One of the goals for senior engineers in my organization is to identify and implement AI initiatives. I'm already familiar with and actively using MCPs, AI agents, plugins etc. While they've been useful, it feels like most people in my company are already working on this. I was curious whether your organization has built or adopted any AI powered tools, workflows, or use cases that have delivered meaningful business value and could potentially be adapted by other organizations as well.
