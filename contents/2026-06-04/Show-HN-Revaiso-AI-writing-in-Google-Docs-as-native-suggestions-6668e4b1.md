AI-editing suggestions for Google Docs.
Revaiso turns your editing instructions into Google Docs suggestions, so you review and approve every change.
** How it works: **
- Select text (or place the cursor) and click the Revaiso icon.
- Describe how you want the text revised.
- Revaiso applies AI output as native Google Docs suggestions. Accept or reject each change in Suggesting mode.
** Benefits: **
- Stay in control: nothing is applied silently—you approve every edit.
- Work the way you already edit Docs: suggestions, comments, and your normal review flow.
- Revise a selection or a whole document; choose word-, sentence-, or selection-level diffs.
- Use built-in prompts or save your own for tone, clarity, translation, and more.
- Start quickly with included hosted edits, or connect your own API key in Settings.
Revaiso is built for writers, editors, students, and teams who want AI help without losing editorial control in Google Docs.

- Supports bullet points
- Supports headers
