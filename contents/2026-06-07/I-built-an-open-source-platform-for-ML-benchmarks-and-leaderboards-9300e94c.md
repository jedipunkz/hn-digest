Build, run, and
share ML benchmarks.
Pick a dataset, define a leaderboard, upload predictions,

Three steps from idea to ranked leaderboard.
Use your own data or pick one from the public catalog.
Images, scalars, depth maps, audio — BenchHub handles
the common ML data types.
Pick which dataset fields submissions are scored against,
assign each one a role (input vs ground truth), then attach
the metrics and visualisations to run on every submission.
Send your predictions, BenchHub scores them, and
your run lands on the leaderboard next to
everyone else's.
Every dataset and leaderboard is either public or private. Generous
storage on either side.
Listed on /datasets and /leaderboards for everyone
Anyone can submit predictions and appear on the board
Counted against your 50 GB public quota — covers dataset bytes, LB materialisations, and submissions
Visible only to you — not listed anywhere
Iterate without the world watching; Publish with one click when ready
Counted against your 10 GB private quota — separate from public
Most submissions in the last 30 days.
Sign in with GitHub, Google, or a passwordless email code.
We only see your name, email, and avatar.
