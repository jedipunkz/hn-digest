Compare how good LLMs are across languages. There is a website and cli to try with the models/languages you care about.
See the repo for more information: https://github.com/inimaz/mothertoken

Different models spend tokens differently across languages.
The bundled benchmark covers a curated subset of tokenizers. Use the CLI to compare additional Hugging Face tokenizer refs against the languages and prompts you care about.
