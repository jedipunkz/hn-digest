Share X LinkedIn Reddit Hacker News Facebook Business What does Zentral do?
Zentral centralizes the core systems SMEs use to run day-to-day operations—so teams stop juggling spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads, and disconnected tools. It combines ERP and CRM capabilities in one enterprise suite tailored to how Latin American and Spanish businesses operate.
The suite includes 8 apps covering People, Finance, Commerce (Sales/POS/customers), Supply (inventory and purchasing), Assets (maintenance and equipment), Workplace (drive, tasks, forms), Insights (dashboards and executive reporting), and Admin (security and governance). You can start with a few modules and add more as your business grows.
A cross-app copilot (Zentral AI) connects to your modules to help you understand what’s happening, generate reports in natural language, and surface proactive alerts—like cash flow thresholds or overdue tasks. Implementation is designed to be fast, with an Indrox team that supports setup and customization around your real workflows.
How long does it take to implement Zentral?
On average, implementation takes about 3 weeks from project kickoff. Timing depends on the number of modules you activate and how complex your operation is, with the Indrox team supporting the process.
Can I start with only some apps instead of the full suite?
Yes. Zentral is modular, so you can buy and activate apps separately and connect them within the suite. Many teams begin with Commerce + Workplace and add Finance, People, or Supply as they grow.
Does Zentral integrate with accounting systems and SUNAT reporting?
Yes. Zentral integrates with main accounting systems in the Peruvian market and can generate the reports SUNAT requires.
What is Zentral AI, and does it work across modules?
Zentral AI isn’t a standalone chatbot—it operates across the apps in your suite. It can provide daily summaries, generate reports in natural language, and send proactive alerts based on your operational data.
Is Zentral customizable for unique workflows?
Yes. The Indrox team can build custom submodules and flows on top of the standard apps, adapting permissions, processes, and reports to match how your business operates.
Is there Spanish support for LATAM businesses?
Yes. Zentral includes Spanish/English support, and the Indrox team operates from Peru to handle local requirements and realities for LATAM SMBs.
