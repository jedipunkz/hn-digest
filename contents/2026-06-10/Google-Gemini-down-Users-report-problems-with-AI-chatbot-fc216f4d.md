News Sports Lifestyle Food Real Estate Advertise Obituaries eNewspaper Legals TECHNOLOGY Is Google Gemini down? Users report problems with AI chatbot
A growing number of Google Gemini users have reported issues with the AI chatbot.
As of 7:41 a.m. ET on Wednesday, June 10, Downdetector.com had logged 746 reports of issues with Google Gemini.
On social media, some users reported receiving "Error Code 1076" or "Error Code 1099" when trying to use the website. One user said chats were failing to load .
Gemini, which launched in 2023 , is an AI-powered conversational assistant accessible online and via a mobile app. The chatbot is integrated across Google Search, Workspace, One, and Cloud.
The generative AI program can help users with tasks such as writing, brainstorming, researching, summarizing information, and generating images, according to Google .
Google did not immediately respond to a request for comment. A timeline for when the reported errors will be fixed is unknown.
The Asbury Park Press contributed to this report.
