What we’re building with Claude Corps
Impact. The organizations communities count on—from conservation groups to museums—deserve to realize AI’s full potential, but sometimes lack the resources to do so.
Growth. Claude Corps trains early-career talent to build AI tools and systems that extend the missions of organizations doing some of the world’s most important work.
Agency. Fellows gain lasting skills and experience as they shape the future of purpose-driven work, while organizations build new capabilities for the AI era.
Claude Corps is launching as a collaboration between Anthropic, CodePath , and Social Finance .
