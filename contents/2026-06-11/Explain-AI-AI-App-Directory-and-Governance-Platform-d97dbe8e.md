AI Application Google DeepMind Gemini is a family of multimodal large language models developed by Google DeepMind, serving as the successor to LaMDA and PaLM 2. It is designed to process and generate text, code, images, audio, and video, enabling advanced reasoning, problem-solving, and task automation across diverse domains. Gemini models are available in different sizes (Ultra, Pro, Nano) to cater to various use cases, from enterprise applications to on-device processing. The models are integrated into Google's ecosystem, including Google Cloud, AI Studio, and consumer products like Bard (now rebranded as Gemini).
