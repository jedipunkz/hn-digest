## Original Extract

Claude Cowork June 2026 usage promotion | Claude Help Center Skip to main content Claude Cowork June 2026 usage promotion
We're offering a limited-time promotion that doubles 5-hour usage limits in Claude Cowork.
This promotion is available for Pro, Max, and Team plans, as well as legacy seat-based users on Enterprise plans. Free plans and consumption-based Enterprise seats are not included in this promotion.
From June 5, 2026 through July 5, 2026, your 5-hour usage limit in Cowork is doubled. Weekly usage limits remain unchanged.
No action is required to participate. If you're on an eligible plan, the doubled usage is automatically applied.
The 2x usage increase applies to Claude Cowork only. Usage limits for other Claude products, like Claude (web, desktop, and mobile) and Claude Code, are unchanged.
Do I need to do anything to get the extra usage?
No. The promotion applies automatically. You'll see higher limits reflected in your Cowork usage without any changes to your account settings.
Does this change my weekly usage limit?
No. The promotion doubles 5-hour usage limits only. Weekly usage limits remain unchanged.
I'm on an Enterprise plan. Am I included?
Legacy seat-based users on Enterprise plans are included. Consumption-based Enterprise seats are not included in this promotion.
What happens when the promotion ends?
After July 5, 2026, 5-hour usage limits in Cowork return to their standard levels. There's no change to your plan or billing.
This offer is valid from June 5, 2026 through July 5, 2026 at 11:59 PM PT. It applies to Pro, Max, and Team plans and to legacy seat-based users on Enterprise plans only, and excludes Free plans and consumption-based Enterprise seats. This offer has no cash value and is not transferable. It may not be combined with other offers.
