## Original Extract

FTX held a diluted 7.84% stake in Anthropic, according to Reuters. Anthropic’s latest reported valuation is around $965B. That implies the former FTX stake would be worth about $75B before further dilution. FTX’s customer shortfall was roughly $8B to $9B.
