仕組み
LinkedIn、Twitter/X、YouTube、またはその他のサイトにコメントを入力します。テキスト ボックスの近くに、検出されたトーンを示す小さなバッジが表示されます。クリックしてご覧ください
それはなぜですか - そして、あなたのコメントが厳しいまたは有害に聞こえる場合は、ワンクリックでコピーできる、より親切な書き換えの提案が即座に表示されます。

Check comment tone with Chrome's on-device AI. Get kinder rewrite suggestions before you post. Private — no data sent anywhere.
Comment Vibe uses Chrome's built-in AI (Gemini Nano) to analyse the tone of your comments in real time — before you hit Post.
HOW IT WORKS
Type a comment on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube, or any other site. A small badge appears near your text box showing the detected tone. Click it to see
why — and if your comment sounds harsh or toxic, get an instant, kinder rewrite suggestion you can copy in one click.
SENTIMENT LABELS
😊 Positive — Constructive and friendly
😐 Neutral — Balanced and factual
😕 Negative — May come across as harsh or critical
🚫 Toxic — Contains aggressive or harmful language
WHY COMMENT VIBE?
• Catch negativity before you regret posting it
• Improve your online communication habits
• Get AI-powered rewrite suggestions instantly
• Works on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, YouTube, Reddit, and more
100% PRIVATE — NO DATA LEAVES YOUR DEVICE
All analysis is done locally using Chrome's on-device Gemini Nano model. Your comments are never sent to any server. No account required.
REQUIREMENTS
• Chrome 127 or later
• Chrome's built-in Prompt API enabled (chrome://flags → search "Prompt API" → Enabled, then chrome://components → update Optimization Guide On Device
Model)
