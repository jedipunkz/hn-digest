ビルドします。プロンプトまたはドキュメントから完全なラベル設定に進みます。 Merlin はラベル スキーマを定義し、ラベル付けインターフェイスをカスタマイズし、ユースケースに沿ったレビュー ステージを設定します。そのため、白紙の状態から作業するのではなく、即座にプロジェクトを開始できます。

最適化する 。モデルのパフォーマンスを妨げている問題を見つけて修正します。 Merlin は、問題を見つけた瞬間にデータとワークフローを修正できる Encord 内を案内します。

Introducing Merlin: The Agentic Intelligence Layer for Encord
Software is changing faster than ever. Interfaces are becoming more conversational and intent-driven, and iteration cycles are rapidly getting shorter. The next generation of AI data development needs to keep pace with this evolution. Today, we’re introducing Merlin – the agentic intelligence layer for Encord that works for you, wherever you work.
With Merlin, Encord is the first AI data infrastructure platform to natively embed agentic intelligence capabilities in the tools you already live in. Merlin is launching in beta today via MCP, in Claude, Codex and all other agentic coding platforms – with Slack and more coming soon. Getting the data right has never been more effortless.
Manage your AI data infrastructure in a single conversation
Merlin works across the full data lifecycle:
Build . Go from a prompt or document to a complete labeling setup. Merlin defines your label schema, customizes your labeling interface and sets up review stages around your use case – so you can kick off your project instantly instead of working from a blank slate.
Observe. Understand the state of your data at any moment. Merlin reports the metrics and coverage gaps that matter on demand, so you can get a clear read without waiting on a manual analysis pass.
Optimize . Find and fix the issues holding your model performance back. Merlin points you to them in Encord, where you can fix your data and workflows the moment you spot a problem.
Anyone building AI systems knows that getting raw data ready for training is rarely straightforward. Every step has required manual effort before a single annotation is made.
Merlin changes that. With this release, we’re giving teams a new way of experiencing Encord through agentic intelligence and conversation, so they can focus on getting the data right.
Merlin launches in beta, starting with MCP. We're opening early access to a selected group of customers to help us build Merlin and shape what it becomes. This is only the start of our agentic ambitions for Encord. If you'd like to be part of it, please register your interest .
