## Original Extract

Mohit here, cofounder of Clone. We help fashion brands clone any viral reel/tiktok 1:1, with their products, and an AI model. Here's a quick demo of the results: https://www.youtube.com/shorts/91vXGTqe6Os?feature=share Why we built this?
Current methods to create AI first videos are messy. They do help you create an AI video, but the video isn't engaging. Main reasons being:
1. Making it via text to video has its own set of randomness.
2. If you're making it by animating keyframes, it gets super painful to create those keyframes for every segment, and then animate/trim them, and then eventually edit/place them. We're placing the bet on:
- If a video is already viral, just use that as an inspiration to copy it 1:1, and swap in your products. If this is of interest, we're currently taking orders on a service based model. You can reach out at mohit@quinn.live Feedback and suggestions are welcomed!
