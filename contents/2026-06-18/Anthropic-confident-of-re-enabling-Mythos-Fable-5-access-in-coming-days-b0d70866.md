Anthropic is confident in re-enabling access to its frontier AI models soon, according to an executive at the U.S. AI giant, following the White House’s directive to block Mythos and Fable 5 for foreign nationals. “We are very confident that in the coming days, the models will become available again,” said Anthropic's Managing Director of International Chris Ciauri during a press conference in Seoul on Wednesday.
