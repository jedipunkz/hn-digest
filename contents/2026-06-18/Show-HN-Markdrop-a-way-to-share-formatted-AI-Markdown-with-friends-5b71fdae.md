## Original Extract

Share AI-generated markdown instantly. Built for ChatGPT, Claude & team collaboration.

I made this because I got frustrated sending AI copy to friends and teammates, and seeing it lose formatting and structure. I found it worked well to instead ask the LLM to summarise its thoughts into a markdown, and share that instead. I set it up to use the same plugins as llms, so you can share tables, mermaid, LaTeX and more. It's a pastebin for the AI era. Try it and tell me what you think! It's forever free to share public pastes, and I'm exploring team plans to organise brainstorming.
