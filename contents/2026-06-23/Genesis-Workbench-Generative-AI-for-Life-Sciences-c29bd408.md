Accelerate life sciences research with generative AI
Generative AI is transforming life sciences by using foundation models trained on diverse biological datasets to enable predictive modeling, drug discovery and synthetic biology. Integrating these data allows models to generate hypotheses, simulate biochemical pathways and predict molecular structures with high accuracy, advancing therapeutic target identification and personalized medicine. Large-scale language models also aid in extracting valuable insights from biomedical literature.
Using Genesis Workbench as a template, life sciences organizations can:
Deploy pretrained open biological models on Databricks GPU Model Serving
Create life sciences-specific workflows with an intuitive Databricks Apps interface
Run advanced AI workflows with GPU acceleration and MLflow integration
Reduce non-biological workload and focus on research innovation
Access BioNeMo models optimized for NVIDIA hardware for high performance and scalability
