💬 メメックスに質問してください
本当の質問をしてみましょう - 「先週の本のレクチャーは何でしたか?」 — そして、あなた自身の過去の会話から引き出された答えを、あなたの心を躍らせる引用とともに導き出します。

🟢 THE PROBLEM
You've had hundreds of conversations across ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. The answer you need is in there somewhere — buried across three sites, three histories, impossible to search. Your AI history is a goldmine you can't dig into.
🟢 WHAT MEMEX DOES
Memex indexes your past conversations from every supported AI into a single library you can search, organize, revisit, and even question — right from your browser.
✨ KEY FEATURES
🔍 Cross-AI search
Search every conversation from ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity at once — by title, keyword, topic, tag, or note. Smart ranking and synonym matching (search "bug", also find "error", "crash", "issue"). Filter to one AI with provider tabs, or search them all.
🗂️ Organize your history
Let AI sort your chats into clean thematic groups, or build your own. Add tags and notes, nest sub-groups, star favorites, and operate on many conversations at once with bulk move / tag / delete.
💬 Ask Memex
Ask a real question — "what was that book rec from last week?" — and get an answer drawn from your own past conversations, with citations that jump you back to the source chat. No relevant match = no charge.
📝 AI summaries
Get a 2-3 sentence recap of any conversation, stored and reusable. Drop it into a new chat as context in one click.
⚡ Prompts, chains & sessions
Save your best prompts and multi-step chains, and paste them into any AI with one click. Pin conversations and messages to an active session and restore them when you come back.
📤 Export & sync
Export your whole library to Markdown, PDF, JSON, or Notion. Tags, notes, groups, and prompts sync across all your devices.
