## Original Extract

Get paid by AI agents for your Substack content.

Hi HN, I’m building Drip. It’s an API that lets AI agents access premium finance newsletters on a pay-per-use basis. Agents will not subscribe to newsletters like humans do. A person subscribes for the ongoing relationship. An agent usually needs one narrower thing: the exact piece of research that helps answer a question right now. We just launched a stock-picks endpoint for what many people are calling vibe investing / agentic investing. Using Drip, an agent can generate a cited report on the latest stock picks and recommendations from top premium finance writers. You can generate a stock-picks report here: https://dripstack.xyz/stock-picks Or give your agent access here: https://dripstack.xyz/?tab=agents Every time Drip answers a question, the authors whose work is referenced get paid on a pay-per-use basis. This is early, and definitely not financial advice. I’d love feedback from anyone building agents, financial research tools, or trading workflows.
