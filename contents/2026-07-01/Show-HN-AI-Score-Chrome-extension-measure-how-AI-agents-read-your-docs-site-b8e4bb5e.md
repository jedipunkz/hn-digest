## Original Extract

Instantly score how ready any documentation site is for AI agents — graded on the open AFDocs standard, from your toolbar.

I built this free chrome extension to get a website's AI score and help you fix it. In the backend it uses AFDocs, which is an amazing AI analysis tool that "measure(s) how well AI agents can read, navigate, and use your documentation site." From the score (number and letter grade) you can then get more detail, which can be sent to your AI agent (click to copy) so you can fix any issues. [1] https://afdocs.dev/

Instantly score how ready any documentation site is for AI agents — graded on the open AFDocs standard, from your toolbar.
Can AI agents actually read your docs?
AI assistants, LLMs, and autonomous agents increasingly rely on documentation to
answer questions and get work done. But most docs sites were built for humans,
not machines — and agents quietly struggle with them.
Jamdesk AI Score grades any documentation site on how agent-ready it is, in
seconds, right from your toolbar.
HOW IT WORKS
1. Open any documentation site.
2. Click the Jamdesk AI Score icon.
3. Get an instant 0–100 score and letter grade.
4. Click "Get Details" for the full breakdown — what's working, what's holding
the score down, and how to fix it.
WHAT IT CHECKS (the open AFDocs standard)
• llms.txt — the emerging standard for guiding AI agents
• Markdown availability — clean, parseable content
• Site structure & navigation — can an agent find its way?
• URL stability — predictable, durable links
…and more.
WHY IT MATTERS
Agent-readable docs get cited more often, answer more questions correctly, and
turn AI assistants into a discovery channel. A low score means agents are
leaving your content on the table.
Free. No sign-up required. Works on any public documentation site.
PRIVACY
The extension only acts when you click. The URL of the page you choose to score
is sent to Jamdesk's servers to run the analysis, and is cached briefly
(~15 minutes) to speed up repeat checks. We don't track your browsing, read page
contents, or run on any site automatically.
Privacy policy: https://jamdesk.com/privacy
