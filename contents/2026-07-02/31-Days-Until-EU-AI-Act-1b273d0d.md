Most people building AI products have no idea how widely the EU AI Act applies - until they discover they’re already on the hook. The law doesn’t just target big tech or enterprise systems; it applies whether you’re a solo developer, a small start-up, or an established company offering an AI‑enabled product or service in the EU. Even simple apps can fall under regulated or high‑risk categories, and the Act requires specific documentation, risk controls, transparency steps, and evidence that your system is safe and compliant. Missing these obligations isn’t a small oversight: it can lead to fines, forced changes, product restrictions, or being blocked from the EU market entirely. This Compliance Starter Pack gives builders of all sizes the practical templates, documentation structures, and guidance they need to hit the ground running with EU AI Act compliance. It helps you get started fast, avoid inevitable non‑compliance, and bring your AI product into alignment with the Act without needing a legal team or weeks of research to decode the requirements.
