Verify selected AI knowledge claims with Spark KNE.
Spark KNE Verify helps users inspect selected AI and web knowledge claims across daily tools.
The extension sends selected text to Spark KNE only after explicit user action, then returns a cautious trust state based on source evidence and KNE Trust Policy. Version 0.1.2 shows a visible data-use disclosure and privacy-policy link before login, registration, verification, feedback, issue reporting, debug-token saving, or KNE contribution can send data.
Current trust states include:
- Source supported
- Evidence insufficient
- Review needed
- Conflicted
- Outdated
- High risk
- Not knowledge
Users can also open source details, send feedback, or submit source hints, challenges, and corrections to the KNE review queue. User submissions do not directly change KNE Core data; they require review.
Privacy summary:
- Spark verifies only text the user actively selects or sends through the extension.
- Spark does not monitor clipboard content.
- Spark does not run background conversation recording.
- Submitted verification text, source URL, platform label, contribution content, feedback, and blocking issue reports are sent to https://knespark.com for KNE verification, beta operation, review, security, and product improvement.
- The privacy policy is available at https://knespark.com/privacy.
