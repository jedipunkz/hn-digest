Surge is an AI growth assistant for early-stage founders. You paste your startup's URL once, and Surge builds a reusable startup profile, then turns it into a weekly, multi-platform content plan with platform-native posts for TikTok/Reels, LinkedIn, Reddit, and X.
Surge generates platform-native content for four channels from a single launch update: a TikTok/Reels hook and script, a LinkedIn founder story, a Reddit discussion angle, and an X (Twitter) thread structure.
Paste your product URL once. Surge extracts your positioning, pain points, hooks, and voice into a saved startup profile, then generates a full week of posts laid out as a calendar (for example Mon TikTok, Tue LinkedIn, Thu Reddit, Fri X) that you can edit, regenerate, copy, and save from the dashboard.
