Free preview: turn 1 piece of content into 3 ready-to-publish posts.
Content Repurposer Lite is a free Claude plugin that turns one long piece of content — a blog post, a podcast transcript, a video transcript, or a newsletter edition — into 3 ready-to-publish short-form pieces : an X/Twitter thread, a LinkedIn post, and an Instagram caption.
Paste your long-form content and say "repurpose this." You'll be asked a quick question about tone (casual, professional, or bold), and you'll get:
An X/Twitter thread (8-12 tweets)
An Instagram caption with hashtags
The skill automatically detects whether your content is in English or Spanish and replies in that language.
Install the plugin — add the content-repurposer-lite folder as a plugin in your Claude Cowork session, or install it via /plugin marketplace add and /plugin install in Claude Code.
Paste your content and say "Repurpose this."
Pick a tone when asked (casual, professional, or bold) and get your 3 pieces.
Want all 10 pieces, in your own voice?
This free version demonstrates the flow with 3 pieces and a one-off tone choice. The full Content Repurposer adds:
10 pieces instead of 3 (adds carousel, TikTok/Reel script, YouTube Short script, newsletter snippet, quote cards, and a poll/conversation post)
A one-time voice setup that captures how YOU actually write, applied and verified across every piece
Saved output files and the ability to regenerate individual pieces
Full bilingual documentation and worked examples
Get the full version: https://getcreatorforge.gumroad.com/l/content-repurposer
