Current AI – Open Source AI Gap Map
Version 0.1
We are building a public option for AI that is open, auditable, and in service of people over profit. However, the open source AI stack has gaps. Now, together, we can close them.
Building on work from leading open source AI experts at the Columbia Convening, MOF , Hugging Face and others, we undertook an ambitious effort to map the open source AI stack to understand what’s missing. We evaluated over 24,626 projects from foundation models through inference backends, assessing projects across openness, capability, and adoption.
