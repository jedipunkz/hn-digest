Orion — improved on Phoenix with cleaner separation and a new direct synthesis approach for better vocal and instrumental extraction.
Perseus — introduced transformer-based processing and delivered better vocal isolation than previous models.
Andromeda — LALAL.AI's most advanced network, built for cleaner vocal separation, more consistent results across different tracks, and faster processing.
In general, newer neural networks offer stronger accuracy and stability, but the final result still depends on the source audio, which is why comparing stem previews remains the best way to choose.
AI stem separation is only as good as the model behind it and the quality of the source track. Yes, the output format can be changed before full processing begins. By default, the output file keeps the same format as the uploaded file, but you can select a different format after the previews appear.
How to change the output format in LALAL.AI:
Open the LALAL.AI Stem Splitter in your browser.
In the upload widget, select the stem you want to remove or extract.
Click Select Files and upload your audio or video file.
Wait for the separated track previews to appear.
Under the previews, click the arrow next to the Split in Full button.
Choose the output format you need from the drop-down list.
Start full processing and download the result in the selected format.
Supported audio output formats include MP3, OGG, AAC, AIFF, WAV, and FLAC. For video files, the output can remain in the original video format or be converted to one of the supported audio formats.
Yes, if your recording contains too much echo or reverb, you can reduce it by enabling the De-echo setting before processing your file in full.
Follow these steps to reduce echo in vocals and voice recordings with LALAL.AI:
Open the LALAL.AI Stem Splitter in your browser.
In the upload widget, select either Vocal and Instrumental or Voice and Noise .
Click the settings icon in the top-right corner of the upload widget.
In the settings list, find De-echo .
Turn on the switch next to this setting.
Click Select Files and upload your audio or video file.
Wait for the preview to appear.
Listen to the result and check whether the voice sounds cleaner.
Process the file in full and download the output with reduced echo and reverb.
