A community tool to detect the use of AI-generated content on the internet through polling.

Hey guys! With an increase in AI-generated content (a.k.a "Slop"), I made a Chrome extension to help people identify it online. It's sort of a like a rating system on how "slop"-y a page is, and you get to see other people's ratings to determine if the content is AI or not. Go ahead and check it out on the Chrome Web Store, and let me know what you think!

A community tool to detect the use of AI-generated content on the internet through polling.
ABOUT:
AI-generated content is rising (often dubbed "slop"). And while there's debate as to how much space AI content should share with human-made content, people have a right to know what they're consuming.
If you've ever felt like there's just too much AI content online, you can help others identify it using SlopWatch, a community-driven Chrome extension.
Unlike many other extensions, SlopWatch doesn't itself use AI, rather it relies on internet-savvy people such as yourself to pick out content that's AI-generated.
HOW IT WORKS:
From any webpage, simply open the extension and sign in with your Google account to rate a webpage. If you believe that a page contains AI content, you can include special tags that specify exactly which kinds of AI content you believe are present on the page.
Ratings are aggregated and converted to a Slop Score that indicates the likelihood that the content is AI-generated, with some leeway for creators who are honest about it.
A popup will appear on the bottom right whenever you load a page that's been rated by other users, so that you don't have to open the extension to check.
SUPPORT:
If you'd like to contribute to the watch in other ways, you can subscribe to it through the extension. If there's enough interest, I plan on developing new features, and especially premium features for those subscribed.
You can also make one-time donations to my "Buy Me A Coffee" page (https://buymeacoffee.com/hademapps).
Join the watch, and let's make the web more transparent.
