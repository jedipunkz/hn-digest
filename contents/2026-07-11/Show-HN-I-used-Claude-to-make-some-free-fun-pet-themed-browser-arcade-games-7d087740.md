砂漠のヤスデを操縦し、種や虫を食べて長く成長し、お腹を空かせたフトアゴヒゲトカゲに食べられないようにしましょう。追いかけてくると、地下に潜って逃げます。どこまで大きくなれるでしょうか?

## Original Extract

Free browser games for animal lovers! Ride a unicycle in Wheelie Pets, grow a millipede in Millipede Munch, defend your yard in Critter Co-op, or escape the lab in Lab Hamster. No download needed.

I created https://whatpetshouldiget.com to help my kid understand the responsibilities of pet ownership. It just grew from there and I wanted to make it a destination site where people can learn and have fun. My kid helped me design four pet themed arcade games (done using Fable). Drawing lots of inspiration from existing titles, with some original tweaks, and incorporating of their suggestions along the way. We pulled these together and you can try them out here: https://whatpetshouldiget.com/play.html There's loads of room for improvement (mobile playability is something I'm targeting now), and I'd welcome any suggestions. Also wondering if it's worth trying to get these into the app/play store. Thank you!

Hop on a unicycle as a dog, cat, hamster, parrot, frog or turtle — each rides differently! Pedal, lean and jump across hand-drawn tracks from the backyard to the moon, then draw your own in the sandbox.

Place your pet defenders — chickens make eggs, cats snipe, the dragon bites up close — and stop waves of garden pests before they reach your home. Three levels with new pets and tougher bugs.
Play the sneaky hamster: grab the keys and break out of the lab before you're caught — or play a mouse, do the task minigames, and stop the hamster escaping. Explore the lab through a fog-of-war view.
