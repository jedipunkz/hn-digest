Your AI. Your Mac. Your data.
Dari AI is a complete AI assistant that lives on your Mac. A capable multimodal model comes with the app - it downloads on first launch, then runs entirely on your Mac. No account. No cloud. No subscription. No setup.
WORKS INSTANTLY, WORKS OFFLINE
The on-device model runs entirely on your Mac on a built-in engine optimized for Apple silicon. Use it without limits, free, forever - even with Wi-Fi off. It understands images too: drop in a screenshot or a photo and ask about it.
TALKS AND LISTENS
Voice mode turns Dari AI into a hands-free assistant. Speech recognition runs fully on-device, replies are spoken aloud in the language you used, and you can interrupt mid-sentence with a single click.
DOES REAL WORK
With your permission at every step, Dari AI reads and writes files in a folder you choose, runs shell and git commands, sees your screen, remembers what matters between sessions, and understands whole codebases with the built-in CodeGraph engine.
SPEAKS YOUR LANGUAGE
14 interface languages, switchable live: English, Kazakh, Russian, Ukrainian, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, German, French, Spanish, Norwegian, Swedish, Italian. Voice replies follow the language you speak.
YOUR MODELS, YOUR PROVIDERS (CUSTOM MODELS)
A single one-time purchase unlocks every other model: download models from Hugging Face, import your existing LM Studio or Ollama libraries in place without re-downloading anything, or bring your own API key for OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Mistral and other providers.
