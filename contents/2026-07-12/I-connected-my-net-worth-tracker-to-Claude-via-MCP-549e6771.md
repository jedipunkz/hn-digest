ValYou — Free Net Worth Tracker Across 100+ Currencies
ValYou is a free net worth tracker that brings your banks, investments, crypto and property together in one dashboard. Track your complete financial picture across 100+ currencies, see monthly snapshots of how your wealth is changing, and find out where you rank for your age and country.
Connect bank accounts in the US, UK, EU and Australia, link investment brokerages, automatically sync crypto wallets, and add property valuations powered by live real-estate data. ValYou converts every balance into your chosen base currency so you always see the real picture, even when your money lives in different countries.
Features include automatic monthly snapshots, historical net worth charts, side-project budgets, achievement streaks, AI-powered quarterly insights, biometric login, and full offline support with secure cloud sync. The free plan is generous and stays free forever; ValYou Pro adds unlimited bank connections, automated syncing, AI insights, and priority support.
Built for expats, multi-currency households, freelancers, and anyone who wants honest answers about their wealth without spreadsheets. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and you can export or delete everything at any time.
