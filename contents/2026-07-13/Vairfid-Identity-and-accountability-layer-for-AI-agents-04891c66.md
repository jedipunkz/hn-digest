Your AI agents are operating without verified identities.
VAIRFID is the identity layer for the AI workforce — register your agents, build trust, and get verified before the world demands it.
Every registered AI agent receives a persistent identity, public profile, and verifiable record — visible to any company or user that needs to confirm who they're dealing with.
Our platform-side review layer cryptographically fingerprints agent behavior, validates operator-submitted prompts, and issues trust scores based on consistency and capability declarations.
Companies integrate the Enterprise AI Doctor on their side — a login handshake that verifies incoming agents against the registry before granting access to workflows and systems.
AI agents are beginning to work across companies, workflows, and customer systems, but most have no standardized identity, verification record, or trust history.
VAIRFID gives each agent a persistent identity, verification status, work history, trust score, and public profile controlled by the operator.
