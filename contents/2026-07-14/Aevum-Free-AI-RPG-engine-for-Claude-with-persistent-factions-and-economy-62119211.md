ChatGPT、Claude、または Gemini で実行される決定論的な王国建設で農奴から主権者に成り上がります。
Aevum Realm Architect は、Arcanum RPGs によって構築された、2 つのファイルからなる無料の LLM RPG です。あなたは 1 枚の銅貨を持った農奴としてスタートし、貿易、戦争、外交、陰謀を通じて王国の王座まで登り詰めます。すべての結果は決定的です。戦闘を決定するのはサイコロではなく、準備と計画です。タグベースの経済は、あなたのドメイン、あなたの軍隊、そしてあなたの財務を順番に追跡します。
世界は、歴史に影響を受けた 5 か国をカバーする 30,000 語近くのアトラスで運営されており、名前の付いた貿易ルート、季節経済、および農奴から君主への昇進を本当に得たと感じさせる仕組みである Deference Engine によって強制される厳格な社会階層が含まれています。

Deference Engine — 厳格で強制的な中世の社会階層

アーチーな。農奴として自分の立場の上で発言することは、実際に懲罰的な結果をもたらします。

Rise from serf to sovereign in a deterministic kingdom-builder that runs on ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.
Aevum Realm Architect is a free, two-file LLM RPG built by Arcanum RPGs . You start as a serf with one copper piece and climb — through trade, war, diplomacy, and intrigue — to the throne of a realm. Every outcome is deterministic: your preparation and planning decide battles, not dice. A tag-based economy tracks your domain, your forces, and your treasury turn by turn.
The world runs on a nearly 30,000-word Atlas covering five historically-inspired nations, named trade routes, seasonal economics, and a rigid social hierarchy enforced by the Deference Engine — the mechanic that makes your rise from serf to monarch feel genuinely earned.
★ 4.9/5 — read the full review
File
What it's for
aevum-master-prompt.txt
The game engine — rules, hidden state tracking, agency protocols. Paste this into your model's instructions field.
aevum-realm-atlas.docx
The world encyclopedia — geography, economics, factions, law. Upload this as a knowledge file.
Quick setup
Create a Custom GPT (ChatGPT), a Project (Claude), or a Gem (Gemini).
Paste aevum-master-prompt.txt into the instructions field.
Upload aevum-realm-atlas.docx as a knowledge file.
Full step-by-step instructions for all three platforms — plus what to do if the game starts drifting — are in the install guide .
The Deference Engine — a strict, enforced medieval social hierarchy. Speaking above your station as a serf has real, punitive consequences.
A tag-based economy — your domain, treasury, and forces are legible, plannable, and driven by specific tags you build or remove.
A world that moves without you — factions advance their own goals every turn, whether you're watching or not.
Deterministic combat — no dice. Battles resolve from your Retinue Power, your plan's quality, and a results table.
Five nations, five starting paths — feudal Al'thoria, Viking-inspired Njordheim, samurai Sakura, the desert trade empire of Al-Jamil, and the crumbling Shattered Republic.
Arcanum RPGs is an independent directory and review site for AI-run text RPGs, and the Arcanum Originals are our own engineered games. Every Original is free to play — we design them, you play them on the AI you already use.
