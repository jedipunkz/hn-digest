Browse and search your Claude & ChatGPT chats, ask questions, see your stats — and turn them into skills. All local, in this browser. Nothing uploaded.
🔒 Everything stays in this browser, on this device. No uploads, no account, no analytics.
In Claude: Settings → Privacy → Export data
In ChatGPT: Settings → Data controls → Export data
Wait for the email, download the zip
Drag the zip anywhere on this page — or Import Claude / ChatGPT in the top bar (no need to unzip)
To update later: request a fresh export and drop it here — only new and changed conversations are re-imported.
Also use Claude Code or Cowork? Drag your ~/.claude or local-agent-mode-sessions folder here (or use Import Code/Cowork in the top bar) to index those sessions too.
