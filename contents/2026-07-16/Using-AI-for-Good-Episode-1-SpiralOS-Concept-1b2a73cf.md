This is a demo concept for something I want to make to help people who don't have wifi access at all times or access to teachers or therapists, they can then talk to their SpiralOS and help learn new things from trusted sources only pulling from real books. These real books can be accessed and read by themself inside the app even. Long term would love to get this on a handheld to be able to ship out with a small screen, thinking like a gameboy color or something. Kids could talk to it or type with the buttons to learn and get AI help with things they might not have access to. The AI here is inspired by Pewdiepies Odysseus.
