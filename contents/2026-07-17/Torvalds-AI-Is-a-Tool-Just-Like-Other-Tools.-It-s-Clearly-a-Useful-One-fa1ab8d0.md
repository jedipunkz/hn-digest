I realize that some people really dislike AI, but this is an area
where I’m willing to absolutely put my foot down as the top-level
maintainer.
Linux is not one of those anti-AI projects, and if somebody has
issues with that, they can do the open-source thing and fork it.
AI is a tool, just like other tools we use. And it’s clearly a
useful one.
It may not have been that “clearly” even just a year ago, but it’s
no longer in question today.
There are other questions around AI (like what the economy of it
will actually look like in the end), but “is it useful” is no
longer one of those questions. Anybody who doubts that clearly
hasn’t actually used it.
