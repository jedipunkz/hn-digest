iPhone, iPad AIpine is the pocket toolkit for AI-generated files.
AI tools now create more than text. They generate React components, Mermaid diagrams, CSV exports, SQL scripts, Markdown docs, SVG icons, JSON data, and more. But on your phone, that output usually ends up as raw files with no good way to view, inspect, or reuse it.
AIpine gives those artifacts a home.
Import files from the Files app or share sheet, then open them the way they were meant to be seen. Preview the result, inspect the source, organize your library, and keep your best AI outputs close at hand.
Render and preview:
• JSX: live React 18 previews, fully offline
• HTML: rendered web previews
• Mermaid: flowcharts and diagrams
• SVG: centered vector previews with dark mode support
• Markdown: clean, readable documents
• CSV and TSV: scrollable tables with full cell inspection
• JSON, YAML, TOML, SQL, Python, Shell, TypeScript, and JavaScript: syntax-highlighted source with line numbers
Switch between preview and source when you want to see both the finished artifact and the code behind it. Customize code themes, search inside files, mark favorites, reopen recent artifacts, filter by type, and sort your library in seconds.
Like a compact pocket tool for AI work, AIpine keeps the viewers you need in one place, ready whenever an AI assistant gives you something worth saving.
Works with artifacts from Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, Codex, and other tools that generate text-based output.
