## Original Extract

Hi folks! As a weekend experiment, I created DWS Report. A simple news link aggregator site where the layout is modeled after Drudge Report (while I don't agree with the news style, the simple site style is fun). The engine collects articles from RSS feeds every 15 minutes, has a LLM score the importance of the articles, dedupes via an embedding DB, and then decays article scores over time. The experiment here was attempting to build a more deterministic LLM workflow, and host something on my own infrastructure + IPs!
